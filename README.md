# LED-Circuit  
  
I made a circuit that has 3 LEDs turn on 1 by 1 and then all turn off at the same time made only with resistors, wires, switches, and capacitors.  
  
It works by having a straight wire that attaches to resistors that come off of it. Each resistor has a different value with it getting higher as you move to the right of the circuit. This leads to a delay in the LEDs when they turn on so they can turn on 1 after the other. The capacitor acts as a short term energy storage that leads to the LED slowly going out when the switch is turned off but it also allows for the delay to occur when they are turned on.
